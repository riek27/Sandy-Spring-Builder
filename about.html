Our Story

Building exceptional custom homes with trusted craftsmanship and a dedicated team for over 40 years.

Your Trusted Builder for Life

Sandy Spring Builders is the area’s premier custom homebuilder, known for bringing our clients’ visions to life through exceptional craftsmanship and a collaborative, full-service approach.

With over 40 years of experience, our integrated team—led by industry leaders Phil Lebovitz and Mimi Brodsky Kress—has built a reputation for precision, professionalism, and lasting quality. Our vast portfolio of distinctive homes has earned recognition across the region, including Best of Bethesda every year since its inception.

Our mission is simple: to deliver homes built with intention, care, and excellence—homes our clients are proud to live in for generations.

Our Philosophy

Built on Quality

Every home is constructed with careful attention to detail, exceptional craftsmanship, and the highest standards from foundation to finish.

Client-Centered Approach

We partner closely with our clients, designers, and architects to ensure each home reflects personal style, needs, and lifestyle.

Thoughtful Design

We create intentional, functional, and enduring spaces designed to enhance everyday living with beauty and purpose.
